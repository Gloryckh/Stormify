Welcome to Stormify.

A simple app designed and programmed by Vik Yabannavar. Built with a clean and minimalist design, it helps you relax to the soothing sounds of rain and thunder. Just start up the app, plug in some headphones (or don't), sit back, and relax. Only for Android. Requires Android 4.0 and above.

The Process

Timeline created by writing when certain milestones should be achieved on a calendar.

I draw out a lot of things on paper, especially when looking into interaction design. Lots of wireframing in this step as well.

I start photoshopping and creating all the graphics. Once those are finished, I start on the coding, which takes up majority of the development process. Troubleshooting is the most time-consuming part.

The last step was publishing the project on the Play Store (September 2013).
